Privacy Policy — Daily Work Log

Last updated: 2025-10-21

Developer/Publisher: Daily Work Log Team Inc. ("we", "us", "our")

App: Daily Work Log

1. Data We Collect

  • Work log content you enter (job title, notes, dates). Stored in our backend so you can view/edit across devices.
  • Account identifiers (email/UID) if/when authentication is enabled.
  • Diagnostics (optional): crash reports and basic analytics to improve stability.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, or payment data.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide core functionality: create, read, update, delete work logs.
  • Sync data between devices.
  • Improve stability and performance.

3. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share with service providers under contract and comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Security

We use HTTPS in transit and industry-standard storage controls. No method is 100% secure; we strive to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your work logs until you delete them or your account. Backups may persist for a limited period.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data by contacting us via the Support page.
  • Opt-out of diagnostics at any time (if enabled in Settings).

7. Children's Privacy

Not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in Canada or other regions where our providers operate.

9. Contact

Please use the Support page.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be highlighted in-app and here.
